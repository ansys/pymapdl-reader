Cyclic Model Visualization
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Visualize and animate a full cyclic model.  This model is based on the
jetcat rotor.

First, load the rotor.  Notice how printing the rotor class reveals
the details of the rotor result file.
